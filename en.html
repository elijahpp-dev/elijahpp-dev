Our Mission

The Good Coin is a community-powered cryptocurrency to build farms (tilapia ponds, free-range chickens, rice fields) to feed the poor. 50% of production is donated, 50% sold affordably. With your contribution, we’ll break ground and provide transparent updates.

Our Goals

  • Build a tilapia fish farm producing hundreds of thousands of pounds of food yearly.
  • Launch a free-range chicken operation for eggs and poultry, donating half.
  • Begin rice farming, donating half the harvest.
  • Document progress with photos, videos, and open records.

Sustainability & Community Impact

Fish parts feed chickens, pond water fertilizes rice, and the cycle supports more food. Workers are paid fairly, and the local community benefits most, with plans to scale globally.
